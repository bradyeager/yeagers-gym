Your First Session

What to Expect

Your first session is a free 60-minute intro to the gym, the methodology, and how we track your training. Here's how it goes.

01 Movement Assessment

We start with a brief movement screen to understand your baseline — mobility, stability, and any existing injury history. No fluff, just what's needed to train you safely and effectively.

02 Goal Alignment

We talk through what you're training for — strength, body composition, sport performance, or general health. Your goal shapes everything: exercise selection, intensity targets, and session structure.

03 Live Velocity Training

You'll train with the velocity tracker on your first session. You'll see your bar speed in real time, learn what your numbers mean, and understand how they guide load selection and readiness.

04 Programming Preview

At the end of the session, Brad walks you through what a block of training looks like for your specific goals — periodization structure, frequency recommendations, and how data feeds into weekly adjustments.
