Data Sources & Methodology

Finish time distributions based on publicly available Hyrox race results from 2023-2025 seasons across Open, Pro, and Doubles divisions. Percentile estimates are approximate and vary by event location and field size. Station time benchmarks derived from aggregate community data across 50+ Hyrox events. Run pacing assumes even splits across all 8 x 1km segments. Transition estimates account for movement between stations and equipment setup. This calculator provides estimates for race planning — actual results depend on conditions, strategy, and individual fitness. Not affiliated with Hyrox GmbH.
